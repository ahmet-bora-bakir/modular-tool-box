# modular-tool-box🛠️

A versatile and modular storage solution designed for organizing tools, hardware, and electronic components. This system allows for custom configurations to fit your specific workspace needs.

---

## 🎯 Purpose
The goal of this project is to create a **space-efficient and customizable** tool organization system. Its modular nature allows users to expand or rearrange their storage as their tool collection grows.

---

## 🧩 Components
* **Main Box Frame:** The structural base of the modular system.
* **Interchangeable Dividers:** Removable parts to customize internal compartment sizes.
* **Locking Mechanisms:** Snap-fit latches designed to keep the lid secure.
* **Source Files:** Original **FreeCAD (.FCStd)** files are included for those who wish to reinforce the design or adjust dimensions.

---

## ⚠️ Important Note on Locking Mechanisms (Durability Warning)
Please take note of the following regarding the **latch/locking system**:
* **Structural Integrity:** The current locking mechanisms are in a **testing phase**. Due to the nature of 3D-printed plastic (especially with rigid materials like PLA), the latches may be prone to **breaking** under high stress or repeated use.
* **Material Choice:** For better durability, it is recommended to print the locking parts using materials with higher impact resistance and "flex" memory, such as **PETG** or **ABS/ASA**.
* **Usage Tip:** Handle the latches with care during assembly and operation. If a latch breaks, you can easily modify the thickness or geometry in the provided **FreeCAD** source files to suit your material's strength.

---

## 🛠 Printing & Assembly
* **Infill:** It is recommended to use at least **25-30% infill** for the main frame and higher (40%+) for the locking mechanisms to increase strength.
* **Sanding:** As with all modular 3D prints, light **sanding** on the hinge pins and latch contact points will ensure a much smoother operation.
